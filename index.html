About

In 1993 entrepreneur and candy enthusiast, Beau Bonbon opened his first candy store in Kingston Ontario. Beau’s passion for candy inspired him to open a place that could bring candy enthusiast from across the world together to discuss their universal love for candy.

Beau’s Chocolate Bars 

Each of Beau’s Chocolate Bars  are each hand crafted to perfection by the worlds finest chocolatiers using premium ingredients harvested in the Amazon Rainforest and imported to beaus to be served to you.

Beau’s Signature Popcorn

Beau’s Signature Popcorn is some of the most delicate popcorn to ever exist. Our popcorn is popped in house and seasoned by Harvard grads who got their Phd in Popcornology to create a variety of unique and delicious flavours that can only be found at Beau’s Bonbons.

Beau’s Gift Baskets & Towers

Beau’s Gift Baskets & Towers include a plethora of sweets and are great for any occasion including but not limited to; birthdays, graduations, bar mitzvah’s, doctors appointments, cheat days, funerals and more!
